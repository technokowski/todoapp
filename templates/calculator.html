Based on historical financial data, the stock market always bounces back. The more time, the better the recovery. If a person invests every year into the S&P 500, their investment can increase by a varying percentage. It will have it's highs and lows, but a good average is typically around a 8% return. The results will hopefully get you interested in learning more.
